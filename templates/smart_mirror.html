Smart Mirror

*Still in development could contain bugs

I built this mirror as a fun project for my own personal use. Coded on my laptop and hosted for free on Heroku, this mirror includes a RaspberryPi chip that hosts the mirrors display. I included information that I wanted to see such as stock updates, current weather, and todays news headlines through various free APIs found online. Future updates include connecting to Spotify and todays sports games.


Tech Stack:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, Heroku Pipelines, REST APIs, RaspberryPi OS
